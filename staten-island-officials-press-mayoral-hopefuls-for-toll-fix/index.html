Staten Island officials press mayoral hopefuls for toll fix

Tweet


By Tom Wrobleski/Staten Island Advance

STATEN ISLAND, N.Y. — With relief efforts stalled on the side of the road in Albany, it’s time to open a new front in the battle against draconian tolls on the Verrazano-Narrows Bridge.

The effort would involve pressing City Hall into action, and making sure that those who would seek to be mayor this year put Staten Island toll relief at the top of their agenda.

It could be the last, best shot that Island residents and businesses have at slashing ever-increasing, job-killing Verrazano tolls.
…
Democrat Cathy Guerriero, an Island native running for public advocate, attended Wednesday’s press conference and said that if elected, she would keep the mayor’s feet to the fire on tolls.

“We have to put these interests at the center of every conversation,” she said.
