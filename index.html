What a week! On Wednesday I received The Martin Luther King, Jr. Faculty Award from New York University.

NYU President John Sexton, Provost David W. McLaughlin, and Associate Dean Pat Carey from the Steinhardt School of Education presented me the award "In honor of your teaching excellence, leadership, social justice and community building which exemplify the legacy of Dr. Martin Luther King Jr."

I am grateful for this award and all that it means. MLK said that 'Intelligence plus character – that is the true goal of education.' I believe that I received this award because for the last 43 years of my life, teachers and educators poured themselves into me. My mother and my father were both teachers and parented me with all the superb qualities of a great teacher: discipline, love, equity and passion.
